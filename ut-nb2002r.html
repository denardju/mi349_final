Up There Reveals Their First New Balance Collab.

Up There is a men's streetwear and sneaker boutique that has been in business since 2010. Over the past couple of years the Australian store has been the provider of exclusive new balance silhouettes across the region. It has recently been announced that the store will be receiving its own New Balance collab. The Up There New Balance 2002R is set to be available on uptherestore.com on July 15th.
